# **App Name**: The Mortality Market: AGT Life-Clock Wager

## Core Features:

- Mortality Market Betting: Allows players to bet on which token will have the earliest projected Day of Death. Players stake tokens and predict the Day of Death to win prizes.
- Digital Divination Counter: Offers individuals their projected Day of Death for a cost, using voice synthesis and embedded audio codes.
- Billionaire's Life-Clock Wager Screen: Displays a list of billionaires with their projected Day of Death, prize pools, and opportunities for billionaires to challenge or validate the dates.
- Spin to Win Gates: Features two Spin to Win wheels: one for a Life Airdrop (AGT Coins or discounts) and one for a chance to win access to the Greatest Intelligence Riddle Game.
- AGT Synthesis Kiosk & Prize Redemption: Provides a kiosk for purchasing AGT Coins and redeeming them for the real AGT, offering a clear path to the ultimate prize.
- Voice Synthesis for Individual Identification: Embeds mp3 codes and voice synthesis within the Digital Divination Counter to identify each individual for a personalized projection.
- Billionaire Interaction Terminal: Provides a terminal for billionaires to interact with the system, confirming their status and challenging/validating projected dates.
- Date Prediction: Provides a tool to assist in date predictions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Use a vibrant array of lights that pulse rhythmically throughout the game.
- Celebrate winnings with sudden music, out-of-this-world cheering, and celebratory lights.
- Design the Digital Divination Counter with a sleek, chrome finish and embedded biometric sensors.
